TRAVEL PLACES


Jakarta have more than enough to be called tourist attraction city. Even for entertainment or historical education. three of the most famous travel places in Jakarta are Monas, Taman Mini, and Dufan.













Monas



Monas is the national monument
of the Republic of Indonesia, built to
commemorate the struggle for
Indonesian independence.
RT.5/RW.2, Gambir, Central Jakarta
City, Jakarta 10110










Taman Mini



Taman Mini is a culture-based
recreational area located in East
Jakarta, Indonesia.
Jl. Taman Mini Indonesia Indah,
Ceger, Kec. Cipayung, East Jakarta
City, Jakarta 13820










Dufan



Dufan is an integral part of Ancol
Bay City, a resort destination located
along Jakarta's waterfront, in North
Jakarta, Indonesia.
Jl. Lodan Timur No.7, Ancol, Kec.
Pademangan, North Jakarta City,
Jakarta, 14430
