LOCAL FOOD


Jakartan cuisine or Betawi cuisine has a lot of influence from immigrant countries, such as China, Portugal, the Netherlands and Arabia. The characteristics of Jakarta cuisine are dominant with a savory and slightly spicy taste.













Soto Betawi



Soto Betawi is a hearty beef soup
consisting of chunks of meat and
offal that are slowly simmered in a
coconut milk broth, which is usually
enriched with various spices such
as lemongrass, turmeric, galangal,
kefir lime leaves, and coriander.










Kerak Telor



(egg crust) is a Betawi traditional
spicy omelette dish in Indonesian
cuisine. It is made from glutinous rice
cooked with egg and served with
serundeng (fried shredded coconut),
fried shallots and dried shrimp as
topping.










Nasi Uduk



Nasi Uduk is an Indonesian style
steamed rice cooked in coconut milk
dish, salt, as well as ground lemongrass,
bay leaves, and kaffir or lime leaves
especially popular in Betawi cuisine.
